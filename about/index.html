hello

Hi! My name is Jason, nice to meet you.

I started my product management career at Vodafone building M-PESA in multiple African markets. The service created a new business model for mobile operators.

Afterwards, I moved to Skrill / Paysafe and learned about marketplaces, cards and alternative payments, CRMs and leading agile dev teams. I enjoyed working with our international teams in Bulgaria and Canada.

I had an exciting year as Head of Product with the M-KOPA Solar teams in UK and Nairobi, Kenya. There I learned about product management for hardware and software, IoT / M2M networks, managing strategic change and data.

Currently, I’m building the PalmPay service in 6 African markets. We’re backed by the owners of Tecno, Infinix and itel. Follow our journey on our website

Thanks for dropping by!
